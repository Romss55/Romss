Tulips

Tulips, so bright as day,
In the fields they dance and sway.
Their petals soft like satin,
In colors bold and vibrant.

Red, yellow, pink and white,
Tulips make the world so bright.
They bloom in springtime's glory,
A sight that tells a wondrous story.

With stems so tall and slender,
Tulips make the heart surrender.
To their beauty, grace and charm,
We cannot resist their potent charm.
